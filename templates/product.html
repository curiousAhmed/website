Frequently Asked Questions

How does HR management work?

This is a simple concept. We do all the activities of an HR-department but for less money and remotely, of course. We'll prepare specialized plan for your organization to make sure that your organization and employees have clear goals which both will achieve together. And ton of other boring stuff already mentioned above.

Why isn’t there a checkout page?

Simple, every organization is different and before we take your money, we want to make sure that we can actually bring something to the table for your business. That's why we do consultation first (totally free btw) and then talk about money.

What’s this “Pay-in-Branding”?

Basically, if you have good social media presence, we will offer you 10-15% discounts on our services, and all you have to do is post us on your socials and write a blog post about us. So in essence you'd be using your social reputation as a currency!

Is there an industy we don't work with?

Actually, this is a hard-to-answer question, it all comes down to the qualification of the employees that we'll be working with, along with the general vibe of the company. Get in touch to discuss this in detail.

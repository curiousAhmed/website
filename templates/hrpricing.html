FAQs

1. What makes FRIDAY different from other HR software providers?

Unlike most providers who only offer software, we pair our powerful HR tools with dedicated HR managers who act as an extension of your team. Whether it’s drafting policies, ensuring compliance, or resolving employee grievances, we’ve got you covered.

2. How does the dedicated HR manager help my business?

Your dedicated HR manager provides tailored support, including:

  1. Drafting employee handbooks and policies.
  2. Ensuring compliance with local labor laws.
  3. Resolving employee grievances and HR challenges.

This allows you to focus on running your business while we handle the HR workload.

3. Is HR Subscription right for my startup / SME?

Our Plans include core HR features like attendance tracking, payroll processing and Performance Management. They are designed for small businesses (1–50 employees) that need essential HR automation without unnecessary complexity and financial stress. If you’re looking for a cost-effective way to manage HR tasks, these plans are perfect for you.

4. Is Hiring included in your plans as well?

Yes, our standard and premium plans include hiring support as well, which means you don't have to worry about this critical aspect of running a business either.
